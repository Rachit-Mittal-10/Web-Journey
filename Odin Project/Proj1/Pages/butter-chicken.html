BUTTER CHICKEN

DESCRIPTION

Butter Chicken is one of the most popular curries at any Indian restaurant around the world.

INGREDIENTS

  • Boneless Chicken cut into pieces
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Olive Oil
  • Ghee
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Onions

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the Ghee in the fry pan. Fry the onions in the pan until brownish color
  3. In another pan, heat the oil. Put Chicken Pieces in it.
  4. Add garlic and Ginger in the same pan.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, salt and red chilli powder to the pan. Simmer the sauce for 15 minutes.
  6. Add the Chicken Pieces to Sauce.
  7. Cook for 10 minutes.
