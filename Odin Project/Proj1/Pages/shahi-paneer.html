SHAHI PANEER

DESCRIPTION

This Shahi Paneer is deliciously rich and creamy, making it one of the most popular recipes in Mughlai cuisine.

INGREDIENTS

  • Paneer
  • Onions
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Shahi Paneer Masala
  • Ghee

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cut the Paneer in square pieces.
  2. Pour the Ghee in frying pan. Fry Onions till brown color.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes in the frying pan.
  4. Add the cream in the pan. Saute it for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Add the Paneer to frying pan.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve with Naan.
